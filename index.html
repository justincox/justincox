About Justin Cox

Justin Cox is a freelance writer, a minister at First UMC Orlando, an editor at The Writing Cooperative, and founder of The Other Word.

As a writer, Justin has written for Wired, Film School Rejects, The 30 Hour Famine, The Billfold, Heart Support, The Coffeelicious, The Writing Cooperative, and more.

As a minister, Justin discussed teenage social media usage with Moody Radio and Interpreter Magazine. Justin also teaches other ministers about social media usage in ministry, preventing burnout, and becoming a mature leader.

Justin lives in Orlando with his wife Carla and dog Mac.
