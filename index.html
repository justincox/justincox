About Justin Cox

Justin Cox is a freelance writer, a Minister to Youth and Families, and founder of The Other Word.

Justin has been published in Wired, Film School Rejects, The 30 Hour Famine, The Billfold, Heart Support, The Coffeelicious, The Writing Cooperative, and more. Justin has been a featured guest on Moody Radio and interviewed by Interpreter Magazine discussing teenage social media usage. Justin also teaches other ministers about social media usage in ministry, preventing burnout, and becoming a mature leader.

Justin lives in Orlando, Florida with his wife Carla and dog Mac.
